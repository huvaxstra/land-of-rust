# 🦀 Sponsor The Land of Rust

**The Land of Rust** is a complete, story-driven introduction to Rust programming for children (ages 9+) and absolute beginners.  
It uses playful metaphors (toy borrowing club, yogurt expiration dates, clay molds) to teach ownership, lifetimes, traits, concurrency, networking, and more – all without assuming any prior coding experience.

**Current status:**
- ✅ 20 chapters fully written in Persian (all levels)
- ✅ 12 chapters fully written in English (beginners & intermediate)
- 📝 8 advanced chapters (13–20) in English – planned for Q3 2026 (dependent on funding)
- ✅ Complete illustration prompts for ~100 images
- ✅ Live interactive demo (MVP) at [https://huvaxstra.github.io/land-of-rust/](https://huvaxstra.github.io/land-of-rust/)
- ✅ Teacher’s guide, code of conduct, contributing guide
