- Normalizing complexity tips
- Toy Town metaphor guide

**Target Audience Breakdown**

| Age Group | Recommended Chapters | Adult Support Level |
|-----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| 9–11 | 1–8 | High (co‑reading, hands‑on help) |

1. Warm‑up (10 min) – Review previous code, quick Q&A
2. Story Time (15 min) – Read Ferris’s adventure
3. Guided Coding (25 min) – Teacher types, children follow
4. Independent Practice (20 min) – Mid‑chapter exercises
5. Challenge & Wrap‑up (15 min) – End‑of‑chapter challenge
